DAY SEVENTEEN

My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy - Kanye West

My Thoughts

For this review, I'll shorten My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy to MBDTF.
It was pretty good! I don't think it's good as Graduation, but it's pretty close!
One thing that MBDTF excels at is it's energy - every song, including songs that aren't as quick, like All Of The Lights (Interlude) and Runaway, keep an energy that make this album stand out.
I thought most features were good, the biggest standout being Nicki Minaj on Monster.
While I will admit that MBDTF peaks during the first half, the second half still has bangers, like Runaway and Who Will Survive In America.

Top 5 Songs (Ordered)

  1. Dark Fantasy
  2. All Of The Lights
  3. Lost In The World
  4. Gorgeous
  5. Runaway

Rating

8/10

DAY FIFTEEN

Unorthodox Jukebox - Bruno Mars

My Thoughts

Another Bruno Mars album!
I didn't originally plan to do another one, but Doo-Wops & Hooligans was so good, so I decided to listen to Unorthodox Jukebox.
So, how's this one?
While not as good as Doo-Wops & Hooligans, I still thought it was pretty good! Even more than Doo-Wops & Hooligans, I think the production is great. While good, the production in Doo-Wops & Hooligans wasn't a large focus, in my opinion, other than in songs such as Our First Time or Marry You.
By contrast, many songs, such as Young Girls or When I Was Your Man, are almost carried by their production.

Top 5 Songs (Ordered)

  1. Treasure
  2. Young Girls
  3. When I Was Your Man
  4. Money Make Her Smile
  5. Moonshine

Rating

8/10

DAY ELEVEN

RENAISSANCE - Beyoncé

My Thoughts

RENAISSANCE was an AMAZING album!
I think it's even better than Doo-Wops & Hooligans. It just has so many good songs, and, out of the albums so far, is the album I want to relisten to the most!

DAY TEN

The College Dropout - Kanye West

My Thoughts

*sigh*
The College Dropout! This is often seen as one of Kanye's best albums. I, personally, have only listened to 2 Kanye albums - Graduation and 808s & Heartbreak.
I thought 808s & Heartbreak was pretty good, and I loved Graduation, and after hearing that this was one of Kanye's best, I was excited.
Unfortunately, this is worse than both in my opinion. Still good, but there are lots of things that I don't really like.
First, I feel like this album has too many interludes. Some are good (like School Spirit Skit 1), but others aren't as good (like Workout Plan).
Second, while I love the theme of college, I feel like some songs sound more like Kanye venting than actual songs. Sometimes it sounds like he's speaking in a song, rather than rapping.
But, there are great things about this album as well! As mentioned before, the themes are great, and honestly took me aback. Also, this is honestly a perfect first album.
I'm definitely in the minority here, so I might relisten to this album and maybe I'll like it more.

Top 5 Songs (Ordered)

  1. Jesus Walks
  2. We Don't Care
  3. The New Workout Plan
  4. All Falls Down
  5. I'll Fly Away

Rating

6/10

DAY ONE

Awaken, My Love! - Childish Gambino

My Thoughts

This album was interesting. I like how weird it got, and it was different from any other album I've heard before. Even Bando Stone and The New World (the only other album of Childish Gambino that I've listened to) sounds different than this.
However, I don't think this album was as good as Bando Stone and The New World. That's not to say it was bad - it had really good elements.
The production is insane; probably better than Bando Stone and The New World's. Me And Your Mama is better than even the best songs on Bando Stone and The New World. And I like the story in the album. It's a good album, just not for me unfortunately.

Top 5 Songs (Ordered)

  1. Me And Your Mama
  2. Stand Tall
  3. Redbone
  4. Boogieman
  5. Have Some Love

Rating

7/10
